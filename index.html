Introduction- Chlorpyrifos 50% E.C Insecticide is a broad-spectrum insecticide formulated as an emulsifiable concentrate (EC) containing 50% chlorpyrifos, an organophosphate insecticide that acts as a contact, stomach, and respiratory poison. By disrupting the acetylcholinesterase enzyme in the insect nervous system, chlorpyrifos prevents the breakdown of acetylcholine, a neurotransmitter essential for normal nerve function. This disruption leads to an accumulation of acetylcholine, causing overstimulation of nerve cells, paralysis, and eventual death of insects. Active Ingredient and Mode of Action- The active ingredient in Chlorpyrifos 50% E.C Insecticide is chlorpyrifos, an organophosphate insecticide that is chemically similar to the natural insecticide paraoxon. When insects ingest, inhale, or come into contact with chlorpyrifos, it binds to acetylcholinesterase, an enzyme responsible for breaking down acetylcholine. This binding inhibits the activity of acetylcholinesterase, allowing acetylcholine to accumulate in the insect nervous system. The excess acetylcholine overstimulates nerve cells, leading to muscle tremors, paralysis, and respiratory failure. Application Methods- Chlorpyrifos 50% E.C Insecticide can be applied as a foliar spray, soil drench, or seed treatment. 1.Foliar spray: Mix the recommended dosage of Chlorpyrifos 50% E.C Insecticide in water and apply it to the leaves of plants until they are thoroughly wet. 2.Soil drench: Mix the recommended dosage of Chlorpyrifos 50% E.C Insecticide in water and apply it to the soil around the base of plants. 3.Seed treatment: Mix the recommended dosage of Chlorpyrifos 50% E.C Insecticide with water and coat seeds before planting. 4.Soil fumigation: Chlorpyrifos can be used to fumigate soil before planting to control soil-borne pests and diseases. Dosage Rates The recommended dosage rate of Chlorpyrifos 50% E.C Insecticide varies depending on the target pest, crop, soil type, application method, and environmental conditions.
